About

There is no formal organization that is arranging Alberta Tech Week. Tom Bielecki (@tombielecki) from local startup Own The Night stepped up to design and build the site (using Cool Kitten + Eventbrite API) and AccelerateAB Co-Chair Sean Collins (@seancollins11) and Startup Calgary Director Lloyed Lobo (@lloyedlobo) decided to spearhead the work in getting the first events going. It's now up to the rest of Alberta's tech community to stand up and organize, plan, and promote their own events.

The backbone of Alberta Tech Week is the 600 person AccelereateAB conference that is proudly produced by The C100 and The A100. Learn more and register at http://accelerateab.com/
