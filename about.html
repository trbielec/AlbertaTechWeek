About

There is no formal organization that is arranging Alberta Tech Week. Local startup Own the Night kicked in to design and build the site and AccelerateAB Co-Chair Sean Collins and Startup Calgary Director Lloyed Lobo decided to spearhead the work in getting the first events going. It's now up to the rest of Alberta's tech community to stand up and organize, plan and promote their own events.

The backbone of Alberta Tech Week is the 600 person AccelereateAB conference that is proudly produced by The C100 and The A100. Learn more and register at http://www.accelerateab.com
